    About

    Each month, unwrap audible surprises curated specifically for YOU!

    Feeling stuck with your usual "go-to's" in your current catalog? Let us help you expand your record collection by a few clicks of a button!

    How it Works

    We send you a personalized bundle of records each month based on your preferences provided to us by completing a questionaire which can be updated throughout your membership. Some of these preferences include:

    • Favorite Artists
    • Favorite Genres
    • Classics vs. New Releases
    • Studio vs. Live

    Don't like certain selections sent? Simply send them back - You purchase what you keep!
